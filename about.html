Our Story

From a small family kitchen to your favorite neighborhood bakery

How It All Began

In 2010, master baker Jean Dupont turned his lifelong passion for baking into Crust & Crumb Bakery. What started as a small stall at the local farmers' market quickly grew into a beloved neighborhood institution.

Jean's philosophy was simple: use only the finest ingredients, traditional techniques, and bake everything with the same care he would for his own family. This commitment to quality soon earned us a loyal following.

Today, while we've grown from that humble beginning, we still maintain Jean's original sourdough starter and all his handwritten recipes. Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to ensure our customers get the freshest bread and pastries possible.

Our Mission

To nourish our community with authentic, handcrafted baked goods made from the finest local ingredients, using traditional techniques passed down through generations of bakers.

Our Vision

To be the heart of our neighborhood, where every bite tells a story of craftsmanship, quality, and the simple joy of breaking bread together.
