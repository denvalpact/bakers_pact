Our Story

Founded in 2005 by Mama Nalongo, our bakery has grown from a small home kitchen to one of Kampala's most beloved bakeries. We combine traditional Ugandan baking techniques with modern quality standards to bring you the very best in cakes, breads, and pastries.

Every morning, our bakers start work before dawn to ensure you get the freshest products possible. We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting Ugandan farmers and producers.
